University Institute of Computing, Chandigarh University Chandigarh also known as UIC CHANDIGARH, is a Private Collegesituated in Gharuan, Punjab. UIC CHANDIGARH was established in the year 2012. UIC CHANDIGARH is accredited by NAAC, NAAC A. UIC CHANDIGARH offers admission in various courses like BCA, BSc, MCA, MCA (LAT). Various Facilities provided by UIC CHANDIGARH are Wi-Fi, Medical Support Systems, Gym, Sports Complex, Canteen, Conference Hall, Transport Facility, Library & Information Center, Practical Lab, IT Centers, Auditorium, Banks and etc.
